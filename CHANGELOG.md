# Changelog

## [0.1.0] - 2026-03-25

Initial scaffold with real material science implementations.

### Modules
- **material** — 9 engineering material presets with full mechanical properties
- **stress** — symmetric tensor, von Mises, Tresca, principal stresses, hydrostatic
- **strain** — engineering strain, true strain, volumetric
- **elastic** — Hooke's law, bulk/shear modulus, Lamé parameters, inverse Hooke
- **yield_criteria** — von Mises/Tresca checks, safety factor
- **beam** — cantilever/simply-supported deflection, bending stress, moment of inertia
- **fatigue** — Basquin's law, Miner's rule, endurance limit
